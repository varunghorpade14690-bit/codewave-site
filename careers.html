Data Governance Consultant - Job Description

You will lead governance design for client data platforms by defining policies, stewardship models, quality standards, and operating routines that improve trust in data.

  • Design governance frameworks, data ownership models, and access policies
  • Define data quality rules, lineage expectations, and issue resolution workflows
  • Align governance with regulatory and internal compliance requirements
  • Work with engineering and business teams to implement controls in delivery pipelines
  • Create governance scorecards and reporting for leadership reviews

Preferred Experience: Data governance strategy, metadata management, compliance, and stakeholder facilitation.
