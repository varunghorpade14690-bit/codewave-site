Lead Systems Analyst (Finance & Leasing Platforms)

London | Hybrid | Full-time | Required Experience: 10-12+ years

You will lead end-to-end delivery for enterprise leasing and finance platforms, translating business needs into RS/FS documentation, user stories, and implementation-ready handovers. The role blends deep systems analysis, SQL and data diagnostics, API and integration coordination, and release ownership across UAT and go-live. You will work closely with product owners, architects, QA, and engineering teams to ensure delivery quality, stakeholder alignment, and production stability.

Main Responsibilities

  • Lead discovery workshops and convert business needs into RS/FS, user stories, and technical handover packs.
  • Partner with architects and engineering to shape integrations across REST APIs, ETL pipelines, and core platform services.
  • Validate system behavior through SQL/T-SQL/PL-SQL diagnostics, data lineage checks, and regression analysis.
  • Own UAT planning, test traceability, defect triage, and sign-off with business stakeholders.
  • Coordinate cutover planning, go-live checkpoints, and post-release stabilization under SLA constraints.
  • Drive stakeholder communication across business, QA, and delivery teams, including risk and dependency management.
  • Mentor analysts and enforce documentation, estimation, and delivery standards.

Required Profile

  • 10-12+ years delivering enterprise systems in finance, leasing, ERP, or contract management domains.
  • Advanced SQL/T-SQL/PL-SQL with proven ability in performance tuning and data-model diagnostics.
  • Experience with data warehousing, ETL/SSIS, and integration patterns using REST APIs, XML, and JSON.
  • Strong SDLC and Agile delivery experience with Jira, backlog grooming, and sprint planning.
  • Demonstrated ownership of UAT, go-live, and production support for critical business systems.
  • Excellent stakeholder management and ability to operate under pressure in live environments.
